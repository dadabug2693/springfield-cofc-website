Who We Are

A Community
Built on Faith

Springfield Church of Christ is a welcoming congregation committed to following Jesus and building one another up in love. Rooted in scripture, we strive to be a community where all are known, valued, and encouraged in their walk with God.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or looking for a church home, we invite you to worship with us. Our doors — and hearts — are always open.
